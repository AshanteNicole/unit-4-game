          Faceted Ethiopian opal.

          We usually associate opal with the cabochon cut, or doublets/triplets in which thin slices from veins in nodules are cemented to or between something hard and protective such as quartz. This rare piece from the Wollo area showing a brilliant display of colour has been faceted for setting, though opal is a touch on the fragile side for this purpose.
